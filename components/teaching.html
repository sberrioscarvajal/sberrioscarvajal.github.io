Teaching Philosophy

"Learning begins when the student feels a resonance between what he is expected to learn in class and his own personal experiences."

Pedagogical Framework

Cognitivism & Defense

Based on Cognitivism, I help students organize knowledge into complex structures. This critical thinking acts as a "defense" against misinformation and scams in the modern world.

Problem-Based Learning

I use evidence-based strategies where students tackle open-ended problems. This gives them ownership of the solution and connects abstract theory to their personal reality.

Safe Learning Space

An inclusive environment is where students can make mistakes without judgment. I actively bridge knowledge gaps caused by different educational backgrounds.

Continuous Improvement Loop

1

Formative

Weekly "Exit Tickets" to identify muddy points.

2

Observation

Monitoring engagement & non-verbal cues.

3

Formal

4

Personal
